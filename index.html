Tab 2

What is Assured Buyback?

Assured Buyback guarantees a fixed future resale price for the car bought today. It helps customers buy with confidence because the future buyback price is locked upfront.

  1. 1

    Peace of mind

    Protects the customer from market price uncertainty and future resale stress.

  2. 2

    Multi-tenure coverage

    Gives customers buyback coverage across the eligible 30m - 60m tenure window.

  3. 3

    Easy and frequent upgrade

    Makes future car upgrades simpler because the resale value is already protected.

  4. 4

    Liquidity when required

    Helps customers unlock cash from the car whenever they need to exit within the covered journey.

  5. 5

    No surprises

    The customer knows the guaranteed value upfront, making the resale conversation transparent.

Tab 3

Claim process.

A simple three-step journey: inspection, guaranteed offer, and bank payout.

Inspection

Schedule a quick vehicle inspection on the app when the customer wants to claim buyback.

Guaranteed offer

The customer receives the guaranteed offer after the vehicle inspection flow is completed.

Bank payout

Once the customer accepts the offer, payment is made directly into the customer's bank account.

Important reassurance

The buyback price is fixed upfront as per the applicable rate card and product terms.

Tab 4

Invalid claims.

Claims will not be accepted in the cases below.

Major accidental

Claims may be rejected if the vehicle has suffered major accident damage that impacts condition, safety, or resale integrity.

Odometer tampering

Any mismatch, manipulation, or suspected tampering with the odometer can make the claim invalid.

Structural modification

Any change to key structures such as engine, chassis, apron, or related components can invalidate the claim.

Ownership change

The claim may not be accepted if ownership of the vehicle has changed during the covered period.

Commercial usage

Vehicles used commercially may not qualify because usage patterns and wear differ from personal-use vehicles.

Other TnC conditions

Any other condition mentioned in the applicable TnC can lead to claim rejection.
